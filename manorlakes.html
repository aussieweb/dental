About Us



We are a dental team with three clinics:

  1. Smile Point which services Point Cook, Sanctuary Lakes, Altona Meadows, Laverton and Seabrook.
  2. Wyndham Smile Care which services Hoppers Crossing, Tarneit and Truganina.
